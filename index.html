Our Aim

To empower our learners with the skills required to make sense of the diversities and the potentially confusing complexities. To create global citizens with deep rooted Indian values so as to make a positive difference in the world they live.             

Our Mission

To foster and nurture the leaders of tomorrow by creating an environment for students where physical, intellectual, Moral, Spiritual growth and overall development is given utmost importance. To groom our students to be sensitive and confident individuals empowered with knowledge, creativity and ability to innovate.

Our Vission

To mentor each child to be an achiever in life and to after a comprehensive learning programme that extends for beyond the confines of a classroom.

About Us

Our school is a Co- Educational day school with a strong belief that “An Education of the highest quality is the greatest gift that can be given to the children. Our next generation will need exceptional academic and intellectual creativity to take on positions of leadership and address the many challenges that face our world.

 

We propose to after CBSE curriculum with a well-integrated practices of Indian culture and Indian Education system. We believe that each child is unique and they learn in their own style. Young minds have infinite capacity to understand, grasp and master various skills.
